# Generated reports

Both files here are generated by `packages/ai-factory-kaizen/src/report/generate-dogfood-report.ts`
— don't hand-edit them.

## `dogfood-self-eval.json`

The latest snapshot: one entry per currently-shipped epic's own telemetry (`confidence: "live"`)
plus the three curated Tier B records (`confidence: "retrospective"`). Every report — including
this one — carries a `confidenceLegend` field explaining what those two values mean and why a
`"retrospective"` entry's `timestamp` is a fixed placeholder, not a real observation instant.

## `history.json`

An append-only log of every past generation of `dogfood-self-eval.json`, oldest first — FR-9's
trend-persistence mechanism. **Each entry is a full snapshot, not a diff**, and there is no
deduplication: two generations with identical content produce two near-identical entries on
purpose. "Nothing changed between these two generations" is itself a real, informative trend
signal (see `docs/requirements.md`, Epic 4) — a reader comparing entries over time is meant to
scan for what changed, not to be surprised that most of each entry repeats the last one.
